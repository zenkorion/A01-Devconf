Frequently Asked Questions

Will I get access to all event tracks and workshop sessions?
Yes, access depends on your ticket tier. The Standard tier covers main tracks and selected workshops, while the Pro and Team plans grant unlimited entry to all premium technical masterclasses and networking lounges.
Can I request a ticket refund or transfer my registration?
Full refunds are available up to 7 days before DevConf 2026 begins. Within 7 days of the event, tickets become non-refundable, but you can seamlessly transfer your pass to another colleague or student via our portal.
Are session recordings included in the ticket bundle?
Absolutely! High-definition on-demand video records and corresponding presentation slide decks will be distributed via email to all registered attendees within 48 hours after the conference concludes.
Can I upgrade my ticket tier after completing the purchase?
Yes, you can easily upgrade your ticket from Standard to Pro or Team tier directly from your user dashboard before the event starts. You will only need to pay the difference amount.
Is there an online or remote streaming option available for international attendees?
Yes! We offer a dedicated Virtual Pass that grants high-definition live streaming access to all main stage tracks, virtual networking rooms, and digital workshop resources.
